Requirements for Active Membership:

Wondering if you have what it takes to join us as an active member?

  • 1. Age: Must be an adult (above 18 years of age)
  • 2. Citizenship: Must be a Kenyan citizen.
  • 3. Talent: Must possess talent in music, arts, fashion. etc
  • 4. Commitment: Willingness to put your work under scrutiny.
  • 5. Adherence: Agree to adhere to all the rules and regulations stated in the organization's constitution.
Learn More
Benefits of Active Membership:
  • 1. Voting rights in organization matters.
  • 2. Access to exclusive content and events.
  • 3. Networking opportunities with industry experts.
  • 4. Reduced prices on our merchandise.
  • 5. Invitations to video and photoshoots.
  • 6. Access to customized products.
  • 7. Access to a supportive community of fellow creatives.

Register as an Active Member today to unlock these privileges and so much more!

Requirements for Fun-Base Membership:

Please note that Fun Membership is designed for individuals who want to support our cause but may not wish to get directly involved.

  • Must be a supporter of our organization.
  • Purchased an organization T-shirt bearing the brand HK4L.
  • Highly recommended to register on our website (registration is free).
